About me

My name is Serena, I'm a Software Engineer, feminist, dog lover, pole dancer.
These are the things I love the most and in this website I have a collection of all the projects I have worked on, some posts about tech and non-tech subjects. I think it's a good way of practicing and sharing knowledge.


I am also the founder of a women in tech community in Brazil, Minas da TI, which I'm really proud of it and I'll be sharing some of our events and projects here.

Minas da TI

Previous Next

Minas da TI is a group formed by women who work in the IT area or who work in other areas and have an interest in technology. We have the objectives of empowering women and promoting knowledge sharing. We use technology as a tool to achieve these goals. Currently the group is exclusively for women.

Our community was founded on 2015 and have now over 1.2k women in our community.
